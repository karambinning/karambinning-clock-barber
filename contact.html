Getting here

A minute from the QEH.

Clock Barber sits on the Lynn Road parade in Gaywood — a couple of minutes' walk from the Queen Elizabeth Hospital and an easy ten-minute drive from King's Lynn town centre.

By car. Free roadside parking on Lynn Road and the side streets behind. Avoid the school-run hours if you can — 8:30 and 3:15 get tight.

By bus. The 36 from King's Lynn stops on Lynn Road outside the parade. The 35 and 49 also pass close by.

On foot. Ten minutes from Gaywood Park. Twenty minutes from King's Lynn railway station along the Gaywood Road.
