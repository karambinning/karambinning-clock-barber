In the chair

An honest cut on the Lynn Road.

Clock Barber is a traditional barbershop in Gaywood — a few doors up from the bus stop, opposite the green. We do the things barbers have always done: a proper haircut, a beard you can wear out the door, and a hot-towel head shave that takes its time.

No booking apps required. Walk in, take a seat, watch the clock tick — we'll get you sorted.

The work

Cuts. Beards. Shaves.

We keep the menu short and the chairs full. Pricing is set at the chair so we can quote you for what you actually want — fade, scissor cut, beard reshape, or the full hot-towel.
