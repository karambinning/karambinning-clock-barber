About the shop

The kind of barber
you can set your
watch by.

Clock Barber sits on Lynn Road in Gaywood — a small, properly-run shop where the chairs fill quickly and the conversation moves easily. We do men's cuts, beard work, and head shaves. No frills, just craft done well, on time.

The shop opens early on a Friday, stays open into the evening through the week, and works straight through the weekend so you can find an hour that fits.

i.

Craft

Clipper, scissor and razor — the three tools of a proper cut, used in that order.

ii.

Time

Long opening hours, seven days. Walk in or call ahead — whichever suits you.

iii.

Place

Lynn Road, Gaywood — five minutes from the town centre, with parking nearby.

Three things, done well

The service
list.

No bewildering menu. Three core services, sharpened over years of doing them. Prices on the door — ask when you call.

No. 01

Men's Haircut

Scissor, clipper, or a clean skin fade — taken from a proper consultation so the cut suits the head it sits on.

No. 02

Beard Trim

Shaped, defined and finished with a hot towel. Quick in, sharp out — the way most lunchtime trims should be.

No. 03

Head Shave

A traditional wet shave of the scalp, with hot towels and balm. The reset every two weeks some of you have been waiting for.
