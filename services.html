How it works

No booking. No app. No fuss.

We're walk-in only. Open the door, ask who's last, take a seat. Quiet times are usually mid-morning Tuesday through Thursday — Saturday lunchtime is our busiest hour by miles.

If you'd like a head's-up on the queue before you set off, give us a ring on 07743 337397 and we'll tell you what the waiting room looks like.
